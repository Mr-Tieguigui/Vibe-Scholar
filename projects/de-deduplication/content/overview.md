# Scalable Near-Duplicate Detection in Trillion-Token Corpora

## Definition

**Definition:** Benchmark MinHash, SimHash, and suffix-array deduplication methods at trillion-token scale, measuring recall, precision, and throughput.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Data Engineering research. Benchmark MinHash, SimHash, and suffix-array deduplication methods at trillion-token scale, measuring recall, precision, and throughput. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
