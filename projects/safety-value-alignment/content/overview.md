# Cross-Cultural Value Alignment in LLMs

## Definition

**Definition:** Measure how well LLMs align with value systems across 10 cultural contexts, identifying systematic biases in alignment training.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Safety & Alignment research. Measure how well LLMs align with value systems across 10 cultural contexts, identifying systematic biases in alignment training. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
