# Audio-Visual Synchronization in Multimodal Generation

## Definition

**Definition:** Develop metrics and benchmarks for temporal alignment between generated audio and video streams in multimodal models.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Multimodal Learning research. Develop metrics and benchmarks for temporal alignment between generated audio and video streams in multimodal models. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
