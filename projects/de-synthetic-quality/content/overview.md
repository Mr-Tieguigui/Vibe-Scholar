# Quality Metrics for Synthetic Training Data

## Definition

**Definition:** Develop and validate quality metrics for LLM-generated synthetic training data that predict downstream model performance.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Data Engineering research. Develop and validate quality metrics for LLM-generated synthetic training data that predict downstream model performance. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
