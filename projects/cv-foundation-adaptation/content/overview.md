# Foundation Model Adaptation with 1% Labeled Data

## Definition

**Definition:** Benchmark parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods (LoRA, adapters, prompt tuning) for vision foundation models under extreme label scarcity.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Computer Vision research. Benchmark parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods (LoRA, adapters, prompt tuning) for vision foundation models under extreme label scarcity. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
