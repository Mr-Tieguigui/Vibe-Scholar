# Code-Switching Robustness in Multilingual LLMs

## Definition

**Definition:** Evaluate how well multilingual models handle intra-sentence code-switching between English and 5 target languages.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in NLP & Language Models research. Evaluate how well multilingual models handle intra-sentence code-switching between English and 5 target languages. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
