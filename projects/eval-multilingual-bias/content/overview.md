# Cross-Lingual Evaluation Bias in Multilingual Benchmarks

## Definition

**Definition:** Quantify systematic biases in multilingual evaluation benchmarks that favor certain language families over others.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Evaluation & Benchmarking research. Quantify systematic biases in multilingual evaluation benchmarks that favor certain language families over others. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
