# Sim-to-Real Transfer Gap Quantification for Manipulation

## Definition

**Definition:** Quantify and reduce the sim-to-real transfer gap for robotic manipulation policies using domain randomization and adaptation techniques.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Robotics & Embodied AI research. Quantify and reduce the sim-to-real transfer gap for robotic manipulation policies using domain randomization and adaptation techniques. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
