# Visual Grounding Accuracy in VLMs Under Occlusion

## Definition

**Definition:** Benchmark how VLM spatial grounding accuracy degrades under progressive occlusion, blur, and viewpoint changes.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Multimodal Learning research. Benchmark how VLM spatial grounding accuracy degrades under progressive occlusion, blur, and viewpoint changes. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
