# Scaling Law Extrapolation for Mixture-of-Experts Models

## Definition

**Definition:** Derive and validate scaling laws for MoE architectures, predicting performance as a function of expert count, tokens, and active parameters.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Deep Learning Theory research. Derive and validate scaling laws for MoE architectures, predicting performance as a function of expert count, tokens, and active parameters. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
