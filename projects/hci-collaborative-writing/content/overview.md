# Human-AI Collaborative Writing Quality Analysis

## Definition

**Definition:** Evaluate how human-AI collaborative writing affects text quality, voice consistency, and writer satisfaction across creative and technical domains.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Human-Computer Interaction research. Evaluate how human-AI collaborative writing affects text quality, voice consistency, and writer satisfaction across creative and technical domains. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
