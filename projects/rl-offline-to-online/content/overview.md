# Offline-to-Online RL Fine-Tuning with Safety Constraints

## Definition

**Definition:** Develop a safe offline-to-online RL transition protocol that prevents catastrophic forgetting and constraint violations during online fine-tuning.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Reinforcement Learning research. Develop a safe offline-to-online RL transition protocol that prevents catastrophic forgetting and constraint violations during online fine-tuning. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
