# Human-LLM Judge Agreement Under Ambiguity

## Definition

**Definition:** Measure agreement between human annotators and LLM judges across tasks with varying ambiguity levels, establishing reliability bounds.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Evaluation & Benchmarking research. Measure agreement between human annotators and LLM judges across tasks with varying ambiguity levels, establishing reliability bounds. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
