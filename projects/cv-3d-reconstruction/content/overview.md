# Neural 3D Reconstruction from Sparse Multi-View Images

## Definition

**Definition:** Evaluate NeRF and 3D Gaussian Splatting variants for scene reconstruction from 3-5 input views with quantitative metrics.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Computer Vision research. Evaluate NeRF and 3D Gaussian Splatting variants for scene reconstruction from 3-5 input views with quantitative metrics. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
