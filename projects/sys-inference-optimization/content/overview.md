# LLM Inference Optimization: Quantization vs Distillation vs Pruning

## Definition

**Definition:** Systematic comparison of model compression techniques for LLM inference optimization, measuring latency, throughput, and quality trade-offs.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Systems & Tooling research. Systematic comparison of model compression techniques for LLM inference optimization, measuring latency, throughput, and quality trade-offs. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
