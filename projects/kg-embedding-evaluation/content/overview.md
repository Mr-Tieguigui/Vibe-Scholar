# Knowledge Graph Embedding Evaluation Under Distribution Shift

## Definition

**Definition:** Benchmark KG embedding methods when test-time entity/relation distributions differ from training, measuring robustness to temporal and topological shifts.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Knowledge Graphs research. Benchmark KG embedding methods when test-time entity/relation distributions differ from training, measuring robustness to temporal and topological shifts. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
