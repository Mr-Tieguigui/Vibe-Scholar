# Environmental Cost Accounting for AI Training Runs

## Definition

**Definition:** Develop accurate carbon and energy accounting tools for AI training, covering GPU power, cooling, embodied emissions, and regional grid factors.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Ethics & Governance research. Develop accurate carbon and energy accounting tools for AI training, covering GPU power, cooling, embodied emissions, and regional grid factors. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
