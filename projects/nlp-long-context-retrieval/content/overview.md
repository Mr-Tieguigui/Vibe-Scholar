# Long-Context Retrieval Accuracy in 128K-Token Windows

## Definition

**Definition:** Benchmark how accurately LLMs retrieve and reason over information placed at various positions within 128K-token contexts.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in NLP & Language Models research. Benchmark how accurately LLMs retrieve and reason over information placed at various positions within 128K-token contexts. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
