# Chart and Diagram Understanding in VLMs

## Definition

**Definition:** Evaluate VLM accuracy on extracting data, trends, and relationships from charts, diagrams, and infographics.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Multimodal Learning research. Evaluate VLM accuracy on extracting data, trends, and relationships from charts, diagrams, and infographics. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
