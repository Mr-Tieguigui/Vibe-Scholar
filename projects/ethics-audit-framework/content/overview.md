# Algorithmic Audit Framework for LLM Deployments

## Definition

**Definition:** Design and validate a structured audit framework for LLM deployments covering bias, fairness, transparency, and accountability dimensions.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Ethics & Governance research. Design and validate a structured audit framework for LLM deployments covering bias, fairness, transparency, and accountability dimensions. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
