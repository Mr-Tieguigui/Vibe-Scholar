# Jailbreak Attack Taxonomy and Defense Benchmark

## Definition

**Definition:** Create a comprehensive taxonomy of LLM jailbreak techniques with a standardized defense evaluation benchmark across 5 model families.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Safety & Alignment research. Create a comprehensive taxonomy of LLM jailbreak techniques with a standardized defense evaluation benchmark across 5 model families. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
