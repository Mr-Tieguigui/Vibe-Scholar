# Grokking Dynamics in Modular Arithmetic Tasks

## Definition

**Definition:** Study the phase transition from memorization to generalization (grokking) in transformers trained on modular arithmetic, varying model size and regularization.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Deep Learning Theory research. Study the phase transition from memorization to generalization (grokking) in transformers trained on modular arithmetic, varying model size and regularization. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
