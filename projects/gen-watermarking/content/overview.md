# Robust Watermarking for AI-Generated Content

## Definition

**Definition:** Develop and evaluate watermarking schemes for LLM text and diffusion images that survive compression, paraphrasing, and editing.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Generative Models research. Develop and evaluate watermarking schemes for LLM text and diffusion images that survive compression, paraphrasing, and editing. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
