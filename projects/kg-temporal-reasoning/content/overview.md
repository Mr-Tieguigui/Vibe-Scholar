# Temporal Reasoning Over Dynamic Knowledge Graphs

## Definition

**Definition:** Build and evaluate methods for answering temporal queries (before/after/during/overlaps) over knowledge graphs with time-stamped edges.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Knowledge Graphs research. Build and evaluate methods for answering temporal queries (before/after/during/overlaps) over knowledge graphs with time-stamped edges. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
