# Emergent Communication in Multi-Agent RL

## Definition

**Definition:** Study emergent communication protocols in multi-agent RL environments and measure their compositionality and transfer properties.

## Motivation

This project addresses a gap in Reinforcement Learning research. Study emergent communication protocols in multi-agent RL environments and measure their compositionality and transfer properties. Current approaches lack systematic evaluation under realistic constraints.

## Research Questions

- **Q1:** How does the proposed approach compare to existing baselines?
- **Q2:** What are the key factors driving performance differences?
- **Q3:** How sensitive is the method to hyperparameter choices?
